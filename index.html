About Me

I'm an 18-year-old aspiring Unity/Unreal developer from Edam Volendam, Netherlands, living by the motto, 'Aspire before you expire.' My journey into the world of technology and software development began with a deep love for games and their profound impact on people's lives. Witnessing how games can spark joy, create connections, and even inspire, I was driven to explore the art of programming to create experiences that could touch people on a deeper level.

Additionally, I am passionate about physical activities such as boxing and basketball, finding solace and inspiration in the discipline and teamwork they foster. At home, I am accompanied by my beloved dog and cat, who constantly remind me of the importance of companionship and care.

Now, I'm seeking an internship in an environment that values effective workflow, hoping to further hone my skills and contribute to the world of game development with my creative ideas.
